About Us

Committed to Making a Positive Impact

Language is one of the key aspects for people to connect, serving as a bridge to promote understanding and communication between diverse population. As the language of the modern linked world, English has emerged as a lingua franca. According to Nagy (2016) defines English as a lingua franca (ELF) as a communication method between speakers who use English for ELF but do not speak the same mother tongue. It can be difficult for people in certain countries whose native tongue is not English to learn on new languages.

There are tons of ways to learn English. For instance, watching films and television series, listening to music and podcast, blogs, newspaper, etc. However, some people think is not enough. Struggles to apply the language in real life conversation still a concern. In order to not giving up, motivation is crucial to be maintain. Al-dalawi et al., (2023) proposed to keep that motivation learners tend to practice their English with peers, when they see improvement, their motivation also boost up. In addition, this collaboration builds a joyful and encouraging learning atmosphere, which lessens the process' intimidating nature and increases its appeal.

That is why Talkify is here for you. Talkify help, listen, and learn together as your study buddy. Together, we can.
